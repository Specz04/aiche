About Us

A student chapter for one of the world's leading organizations for Chemical Engineers, AIChE BITS Pilani consists of a group of motivated and dedicated students working towards a common goal- combining the theoretical and practical aspects of Chemical Engineering while working on various projects, and at the same time, spreading the knowledge we gain.

Our student chapter aims to provide a platform for students to enhance their technical knowledge, develop leadership and professional skills. we also ensure that every team member is engaged and learns something new from every task we also ensure that we come up with something new every time and not only promote Chemical engineering but STEM too.

Various teams in our student chapter include: Research and Development, Publicity and Design, Sponsorship and marketing, Web Development, Events and Logistics, International and Sister Relations.

The Road Ahead

Our chapter is open to all Engineering students who are passionate about doing Research in this vast field and are looking to make a difference.
